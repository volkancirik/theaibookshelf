The AI Bookshelf

The AI Bookshelf is a series of book reviews on intelligence for people interested in Artificial Intelligence. There are tons of people out there authoring great blogs on the recent advances in AI. Here, I try to take an orthogonal approach. Starting from earlier books, I review what people have been attempting to solve or formulate intelligence. Since we still do not have a clear understanding of what the intelligence is and how to design or create one, I believed reviews like the ones you find on this website could guide our thinking process moving forward. Here is my academic website. Please reach me out if you have any questions or suggestions.
